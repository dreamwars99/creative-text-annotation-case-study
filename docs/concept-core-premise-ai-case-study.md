# Concept Core Premise AI Case Study

The core premise workflow turns a rough concept sentence into a deeper story rule.

Example concept:

> In a city where names can be bought and sold, people without names are treated as if they never existed.

The desired AI output is not just a logline. It should extract the repeatable conflict rule behind the story.

Example candidate:

> In a society where identity becomes property, losing a name means losing the right to be remembered, protected, or loved.

## Candidate Contract

The private product uses a richer internal contract, but the public shape can be summarized as:

- source text
- task id
- candidate statement
- confidence
- evidence reference
- quality flags
- provenance
- review status

## Review Principle

The AI candidate does not become canonical automatically in the reviewed MVP flow.

The writer can:

- apply it
- edit it
- regenerate it
- hide it
- keep writing without it

## Why This Is Domain-Specific

A generic AI model may produce a summary or marketing line. PlotScope needs story-aware extraction: what pressure, contradiction, cost, or rule will keep generating scenes later?
